Contribute data

If you would like to contribute data to be included in OpenTexts.World, please email stuart.lewis@nls.uk and gill.hamilton@nls.uk for details.

Formats accepted

We can work with MARC21, Dublin Core or TSV (tab-separated) files in the format below.

If your data is in another format, please contact us as we may be able to process and integrate it. We love a challenge!

Tab-separated format

If you are submitting tab-separated data please follow the format below. Contact us if you want to include other data/fields that are not represented in the format - we'll try and adapt our tools to accommodate your data.

Repeatable fields must be separated with a '|' pipe.

Column Heading Content Requirement Repeatable Notes
1 organisation Unique name of provider MANDATORY No So we know which organisation "owns" the metadata and content - this info might be displayed in a facet or in the full record display

The British Library
2 idLocal Local identifier MANDATORY No ID from the local system - so each library can look up their local system if there's a query
3 title Title of the work MANDATORY No For example MARC 245 $a $b or DC terms:title
4 urlMain URL to access the item MANDATORY No The URL that is most appropriate for a user to follow to get direct/immediate access to the content. May be in MARC 856 or DC terms:identifier. This will likely be a 'landing page' containing metadata about the item and the item itself.
5 year Year of publication DESIRABLE No Ideally numeric MARC 008 position 7-10 rather than MARC 260 $c - e.g. preferred to 1884 vs ca.1884). May be in DC terms:date
6 publisher Publisher DESIRABLE Yes May be in MARC 260 $b or MARC 264 $a or DC terms:publisher
7 creator Creator DESIRABLE Yes Author, editor, creator, organisation - any named person/organisation involved in the creation of the work. May be in MARC 1xx, 7xx and DC terms:creator, DC terms:contributor
8 topic Subject or topic DESIRABLE Yes May be in MARC 6xx, and DC terms:subject
9 description Description DESIRABLE Yes Description about the content. May be in MARC 5xx or DC terms:description
10 urlPDF URL of a PDF OPTIONAL No If there is a PDF version of the item
11 urlOther URL of other useful version(s) OPTIONAL Yes If there are other URLs for the content that may be useful - perhaps Google Books URL, or link to the catalogue record of the original
12 urlIIIF URL to a IIIF manifest OPTIONAL No URL to a IIIF manifest
13 placeOfPublication Place of publication OPTIONAL Yes May be in MARC 260 $a or MARC 264 $a or DC terms:coverage
14 licence Licence of the material OPTIONAL No May be in MARC 540 or DC terms:rights

For example CC-BY
15 idOther Other local or external ids OPTIONAL Yes Other identifiers such as DOIs
16 catLink A link directly to the item's catalogue record OPTIONAL No A link to the item in your catalogue or discovery system
17 language language of the resource OPTIONAL No May be in MARC 008 position 35-37 and/or MARC 041.[code list] or DC terms:language.

Repeatable fields are separated with a '|' pipe.
